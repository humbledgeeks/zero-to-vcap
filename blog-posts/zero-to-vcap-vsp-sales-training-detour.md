## The Dual Hat Reality

Here's something that doesn't get talked about enough in partner training.

When I'm in a presales conversation, I'm thinking about business outcomes, licensing, and total cost of ownership. When I'm in delivery mode, I'm thinking about network requirements, cluster design, and SDDC Manager workflows.

Good training bridges both worlds. These accreditations lean heavily on the sales and positioning side â€” which makes sense given what they are. But for practitioners like me, the value is in the translation layer: taking what I know about VCF's competitive strengths and turning it into a technically credible conversation with a skeptical vSphere admin.

That skill set doesn't come from a curriculum. It comes from doing both jobs at the same time.
